## Longer Version

`TheStartupBench` is an executable benchmark project for startup and business decision-making. The goal is to measure whether an agent can operate a company under uncertainty across product, GTM, finance, people, board, and crisis contexts.

The benchmark already includes:

- a stateful runtime
- tool-constrained interaction
- hidden `test` and `fresh` packs
- canary anti-gaming packs
- programmatic scoring
- official eval profile and run-manifest support
- synthetic calibration on the clearest evaluator gaps

It does not yet claim to be a final benchmark-standard public leaderboard. That is intentional.

We want early volunteers who are comfortable helping improve something serious but still in active hardening.

Best fits:

- people who care about benchmark quality
- people who care about startup operating judgment
- people who want to help shape a benchmark before it ossifies

Current ask:

- try the benchmark
- inspect the docs and workflow
- contribute scenarios, tests, runtime logic, or reviewer feedback
- help us make it stronger before broader public rollout
