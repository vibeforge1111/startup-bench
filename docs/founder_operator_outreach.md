# Founder And Operator Outreach

## Short Outreach Version

I am building `TheStartupBench`, a benchmark for evaluating how well AI agents and models make startup decisions under uncertainty.

It is already executable and benchmark-shaped, but it is still in the pre-human-calibration stage. I am looking for a small number of founders and startup operators who are willing to help improve it over time by reviewing whether the benchmark rewards the right behavior.

This is not a request to use AI in your company. It is a request to help validate whether the benchmark matches real operating judgment.

If that sounds interesting, I can send:

- a short overview
- a few example scenarios
- the reviewer workflow when we are ready to run a human calibration wave

## Longer Outreach Version

I am building `TheStartupBench`, a benchmark for startup and business decision-making.

The goal is to test whether an agent or model can actually operate under startup constraints across:

- product
- GTM
- finance
- people
- board communication
- crises and operational failures

The benchmark already has a real runtime, hidden eval packs, and automated scoring. The next important step is eventual human/operator calibration, where founders and experienced operators review whether the benchmark is rewarding the right behavior.

What I would want from founder/operator volunteers later:

- review a small set of completed benchmark runs
- judge whether the actions were actually good
- flag places where the benchmark is too lenient, too harsh, or rewarding benchmark-gaming

What I am not asking for:

- a large time commitment
- proprietary company data
- public endorsement

At this stage I am mainly building the small set of people who can help make the benchmark credible when we open that calibration wave.
