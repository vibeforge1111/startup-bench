# Technical Contributor Brief

## Who This Is For

This brief is for engineers, benchmark researchers, agent-eval people, and technically strong contributors who may want to help improve `TheStartupBench`.

## What The Project Is

`TheStartupBench` is a pre-human-calibration benchmark for evaluating startup and business decision-making under uncertainty.

It already has:

- executable runtime
- hidden `test` and `fresh` packs
- programmatic scoring
- suite and submission artifacts
- canary anti-gaming packs
- synthetic calibration workflow

It still needs:

- more hidden corpus breadth
- more evaluator confidence
- more benchmark governance maturity
- eventual human/operator validation

## High-Leverage Contribution Areas

- hidden scenario authoring for finance, people, GTM, board, and crisis
- runtime/world-state mechanics
- evaluator regressions and edge-case scoring tests
- stronger baseline families
- hidden-pack governance and lifecycle improvements
- contributor and reviewer onboarding improvements

## What Good Contributions Look Like

- small, testable, high-signal changes
- extending existing mechanics rather than inventing isolated one-offs
- preserving hidden-pack integrity
- keeping benchmark claims conservative

## What We Are Not Looking For

- vague "idea dumping" without runnable or spec-backed improvement
- cosmetic benchmark inflation
- changes that make the benchmark easier to game
- public leaderboard framing that outruns the evidence
